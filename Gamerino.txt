Arduino Pro Micro / Leonardo based joypad

MICROCONTROLLER:
	Atmel/Microchip ATMega32U4

GPIO EXTENDER:
	TI TCA9539-Q1

BUTTONS:

LT1, RT1:
	2 x TLLA-72
	OR
	2 x APEM 1FS (5G+1DS RAS)

LT2, RT2:
	2 x TLLA-72 (digital)
	OR
	2 x Bourns 3046L-1-103 (analog pot)
	OR
	2 x Honeywell  SS490 (analog hall)
	+ Eclipse Mag. N835RS

CROSS BUTTONS:
	4 x APEM 1FS (5G+1DS - TH)

SPARE BUTTONS:
	5 x APEM 1FS

HATSWITCH:
	1 x ALPS Alpine RKJXS1004001

ANALOG STICK:
	2 x ALPS Alpine	RKJXU1210006
